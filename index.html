Frequently asked questions

Find quick answers to common questions.

How do I log in to my account?⌄
Click the "Login" button at the top of the site and enter your registered email address. A 6-digit code will be sent to your inbox within seconds. Enter the code on the site to access your account.
How do I add balance to my account?⌄
Once you're logged in, visit your customer dashboard and go to the "Account Balance" section, then select "Add Balance." You'll be able to choose from several payment methods including credit or debit card, cryptocurrency, or manual bank transfer.
How do I view available product stock?⌄
To view stock availability, click on any product to open its popup window. Each variant will display the number of stock currently available. If a particular variant is sold out, it will be disabled. Stock availability is shown in real-time.
How do I purchase a product?⌄
Click on any product to open its popup, select the variant, then tap "Buy Now" to go straight to checkout or "Add to Cart" if you plan to continue shopping. At checkout, you'll select a payment type and complete your order.
How do I create an account?⌄
Click the "Create Account" button at the top of the page and enter your email address. We'll send a 6-digit verification code to your inbox to confirm your email belongs to you. After entering the code on the site, your account will be created right away.
How can I withdraw my balance?⌄
Balance withdrawals are not supported. By making a purchase, you agree to this policy.
How can I request a refund or replacement?⌄
To request a refund or replacement, please submit a ticket and include proof of your issue. Refunds are issued only as site balance. By making a purchase, you agree to these terms.
